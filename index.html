Welcome to O.B. Montessori Center


O.B. Montessori Center is a progressive Pre-K to Grade 12 co-educational school in the Philippines. We offer a strong alternative to international schools by blending Montessori tradition with modern education to prepare students for life.

Our mission is to develop independent, confident, and productive individuals who are ready to succeed and lead in a constantly changing world.

Explore our programs and become part of a vibrant learning community where every child can grow and thrive.
